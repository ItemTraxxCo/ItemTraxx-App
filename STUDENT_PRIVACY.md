# Student Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026-06-11 (year-month-day)

This notice describes how ItemTraxx processes student information when a school or district uses
the service. It supplements the Privacy Policy and the customer's signed agreement.

## School Authorization

The school or district contracts for ItemTraxx and authorizes processing for educational inventory,
checkout, return, and related administration. Students do not create independent consumer accounts
and are not asked to accept ItemTraxx contractual terms.

Where COPPA applies, ItemTraxx relies on school authorization only for the educational context. An
authorized adult administrator must attest to the school's authority before creating borrower
records. The school is responsible for providing notices and obtaining any authorization required
from parents or guardians. ItemTraxx will provide the school the information it needs to do so.

## Information Processed

- generated pseudonymous usernames and generated borrower IDs
- school, district, tenant, and borrower-record assignments
- inventory checkout, return, status, and transaction history
- administrative audit and support records related to operating the service

ItemTraxx does not collect student age, date of birth, name, or email as part of a borrower record.
Student borrower records are stored in the application database and are not independent Supabase
Auth accounts. Students use generated borrower credentials for the checkout workflow rather than
email-based consumer accounts.

## Use and Disclosure

ItemTraxx uses student information only to provide, secure, support, and maintain the contracted
service. It does not sell student information, use it for targeted advertising, build advertising
profiles, or use it for unrelated commercial purposes.

Information is available to authorized school administrators and ItemTraxx operators with the
required role and tenant scope. Infrastructure providers may process data as needed for database,
storage, hosting, edge security, monitoring, email, and support functions. ItemTraxx does not enable
advertising use of student information in the application.

## Access, Correction, and Deletion

Parents and guardians should contact the applicable school or district. The school controls its
education records and can submit verified instructions to ItemTraxx. Schools may use
[itemtraxx.com/privacy-request](https://itemtraxx.com/privacy-request) to request access, correction,
export, archive, restore, or deletion. Permanent deletion depends on an approved retention process
or a verified operational request; automated database retention is not enabled by default.

## California SOPIPA Compliance

ItemTraxx operates websites and applications used primarily for K–12 school purposes in California
and complies with the Student Online Personal Information Protection Act (SOPIPA), Cal. Bus. &
Prof. Code §22584 et seq. ItemTraxx does not use covered information for targeted advertising,
does not build advertising profiles on students, does not sell student information, and does not
use student information for any purpose other than providing the contracted K–12 educational
inventory and checkout service. Other state student-data privacy statutes with similar requirements
are honored to the extent applicable to ItemTraxx's operations in those jurisdictions.
